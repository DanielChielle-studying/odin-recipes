Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These simple chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and packed with melty chocolate chips in every bite. Made with basic pantry ingredients like butter, sugar, flour, and vanilla, this recipe is quick and easy to whip up. After just a few minutes of mixing, the dough is ready to be scooped and baked, resulting in cookies with a golden edge and a soft, gooey center. Perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth or sharing with friends and family!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
